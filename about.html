About us

Mind and Body

Susan Miranda opened Mind and Body Yoga Studio 2019 after years of looking for a studio with that would help advance her Yoga practice. She had been told by many advanced Yoga instructors that a great way to learn is by teaching. Therefore she made the decision to open Mind and Body. Mind and Body Yoga Studio is a peaceful studio in the heart of Stockholm, Sweden. The purpose of this studio is to provide a safe enviroment to practice yoga, wheter this is your first time or if your looking to advance your skills.
